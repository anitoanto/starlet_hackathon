Starlet

Starlet is an innovation contest exclusively for women and other gender minorities, encouraging collaboration and bringing out the best in female technologists. It facilitates meaningful connections and boosts the confidence of participants, creating a safe space for learning, networking, and teamwork.

Starlet is dedicated to empowering women and other gender minorities with a crazy idea and a will to implement it into a working prototype. We are committed to ensuring that participants get a fair chance to put forth their ideas and showcase their experience and talent.

See our brochure here.

This inclusive hackathon empowers communities through inclusive participation. Meals made by local housewives add a vibrant touch, showcasing their culinary skills.

It welcomes people from all academic backgrounds, celebrating their innovation alongside peers. It's a testament of creativity and leadership in tech. Organized for women, by women, this hackathon provides a safe platform and cultivates a supportive community for all.

FAQs
Is this hackathon an online event or offline?
Starlet 3.0 is an offline event held at ASAP Community Skill Park,Kalamassery. Participants are required to be present at the venue at the day of the event.
Does this hackathon has any participation fees for students?
No. The hackathon is free of cost for any student from any college all over India.
What are all the restrictions on team formations for the event?
A team can have at most 3 members and a minimum of 2 members.
What should I do if I do not have any team yet?
Just fill out the application form and based on the skills you have filled out, we will match you with the best team possible during the hackathon.
What are the perks and benefits of participating in this hackathon?
Participants will gain hands-on mentorship from experts from various fields. Mentors will give personalized guidance. Additionally, participants will receive cool swags, stickers, and refreshments to keep them energized.
How to reach ASAP community skill park ?
The nearest railway station is Ernakulam Junction. Participants can take a bus or taxi from the railway station to the CUSAT campus. Metro's are also available from major cities like Kalamassery and Aluva.
